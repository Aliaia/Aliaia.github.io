Ontologies and Knowledge Representation

I design and apply ontologies to formalize knowledge in both spatial and non-spatial domains. This includes:

  • Geo-ontologies and folksonomies to model place concepts and relationships
  • Ontology-based representations for mental health conditions (e.g. OCD), supporting structured analysis and interoperability
  • Developing semantic frameworks that bridge qualitative reasoning, data integration, and domain knowledge
