Prospective PhD Students

I am always interested in hearing from motivated students who wish to pursue a PhD in areas related to my research interests. Potential research topics include:

  • Semantic and Qualitative Place Modelling - Place knowledge graphs, linked geospatial data, qualitative spatial reasoning
  • Human-Centred AI - Audio-based social media analytics, multimodal user profiling, bias in AI systems
  • Location-Based Social Networks - Privacy-aware systems, geo-semantic user profiling, place affordance
  • Ontologies and Knowledge Representation - Domain ontologies (health, geography), geographic question answering
